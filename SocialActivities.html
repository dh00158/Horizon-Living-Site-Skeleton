Social Activities

Horizon Living has daily social activities, check the event calendar below!

At Horizon Living, we believe that staying socially active is key to a happy and fulfilling life. Our community offers a variety of engaging activities for all residents—bingo, board games, movie nights, and bring-your-pet days to name a few!

Residents join fitness classes and hobby clubs to game nights and day trips to local attractions. These moments create opportunities to connect, laugh, and make lasting memories with friends and family.

Whether you're a social butterfly or someone who enjoys smaller, quieter gatherings, you'll find a vibrant mix of experiences to choose from at Horizon Living.
